Pickr AI – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Pickr AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Pickr AI, we may collect the following information:

  • TikTok Account Data: When you connect your TikTok account, we receive an OAuth access token that allows us to post content on your behalf. We do not store your TikTok password.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including which products you select and posts you generate.
  • Device Information: Basic browser and device information for security and functionality purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Publish TikTok posts on your behalf using TikTok's Content Posting API, only when you explicitly request it.
  • Generate AI-powered promotional content for AliExpress affiliate products.
  • Improve and maintain the Pickr AI service.
  • Respond to your support requests.

3. TikTok API Data Usage

Pickr AI uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to post content that you have reviewed and approved. We request only the minimum necessary scopes:

  • user.info.basic – to identify your account.
  • video.publish – to post videos and captions to TikTok on your behalf.

We do not sell, rent, or share your TikTok data with third parties. Your TikTok access token is used only for posting and is never shared externally.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@pickrai.com. Upon disconnecting your TikTok account, we delete your access token immediately.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and access tokens are stored encrypted.

6. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • TikTok for Developers – for content posting. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • AliExpress Affiliate Program – for product data and affiliate links.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also revoke TikTok authorization at any time through your TikTok account settings under "Manage App Permissions."

8. Children's Privacy

Pickr AI is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@pickrai.com
